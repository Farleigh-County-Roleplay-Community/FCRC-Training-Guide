    Farleigh County Training Guide

    Welcome trainers! Use this guide to run your sessions step by step.

    Intro

    Hello! My name is [INSERT NAME], and I’ll be your cohost today. Training is split into Theoretical and Physical sections.

    Instructor: Introduce yourself and explain the training structure.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Starter Quiz (Theoretical)

    Questions:

    1. What does FRP mean in ER:LC, and why is it important?
    2. Explain “GTA Driving” and why it is not allowed.
    3. Why is communication important during roleplay?

    Instructor: Ask questions one by one, explain reasoning after answers.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Scenario Quiz (Theoretical)

    1. You see someone running red lights repeatedly. What do you do?
    2. Another player begins shooting without reason. How do you respond?
    3. You’re in a pursuit but suspect drives unrealistically. Actions?

    Instructor: Read scenarios slowly. Evaluate trainee responses professionally.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Driving Test 1 (Physical)

    Drive from the Sheriff’s Office to River City Bank, following all traffic laws.

    Instructor: Observe signaling, speed, and roleplay realism.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Patrolling (Physical)

    Patrol River City and identify rule breakers. Trainers may act as rule breakers.

    Instructor: Ensure trainees respond professionally.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Driving Test 2 (Physical)

    Drive from River City to Springfield Main Street following all traffic laws.

    Instructor: Focus on highway safety and lane discipline.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Scenarios (Physical)

    • Reckless driver
    • Civilian dispute
    • Traffic collision
    • Bank robbery roleplay

    Instructor: Provide realistic mod calls. Observe decision-making.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Command Practice (Physical)

    • Set a priority timer
    • Check active moderators
    • Issue server message
    • Final command: /load

    Instructor: Ensure trainees know commands and use them properly.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Ending Questions (Theoretical)

    • Most important rule in ER:LC?
    • Handling disrespectful civilians?
    • Why teamwork is important?

    Instructor: Discuss answers and give feedback before results.

    ###### Now we’re done with this section. Let’s go to the next section!

    Results

    Stand in formation. Pass: “Congratulations!” Fail: “Please reapply after preparation.”

    Instructor: Deliver results clearly and respectfully.
