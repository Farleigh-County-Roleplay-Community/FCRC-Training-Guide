Farleigh County Training Guide

Intro

Hello! My name is [Instructor Name] and I’m your cohost.
Your training will be split into two sections: Theoretical and Physical.
Introduce yourself to the trainees and explain how the training will be divided.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Starter Quiz (Theoretical)

Answer the following questions:
1. What does FRP mean?
2. Is GTA-style driving allowed?
3. What do you do if you see someone breaking server rules?
4. What does RDM mean?
Copy and paste the questions for the trainees. Discuss answers together.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Scenario Quiz (Theoretical)

Here are 3 scenarios. Tell me how you would respond:
1. A player is speeding through the city in an unrealistic way.
2. You see someone VDMing in River City.
3. A player refuses to roleplay after being pulled over.
Copy the scenarios, listen to trainee responses, and correct where needed.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Driving Test (Physical)

Drive from the Sheriff’s Office to River City Bank. Follow all traffic laws!
Observe each trainee driving. Look for safe, rule-abiding driving.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Patrolling (Physical)

You will now go on patrol in River City. Look for rule breakers!
Trainers may roleplay as rule breakers. See how trainees react.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Driving Test 2 (Physical)

Drive from River City to Springfield Main Street. Follow the road rules carefully!
Watch carefully for safe turns, signaling, and adherence to road laws.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Scenarios (Physical)

Each trainee will do 2 moderator calls. Examples of possible calls:
- Robbery in progress
- Traffic stop
- Disturbance at a store
- Pursuit situation
Assign 2 different calls per trainee and observe their response.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Command Practice (Physical)

Use the following commands:
- How do you make a priority timer?
- Show me how you call a traffic stop.
- Use the load command to return everyone to the meeting room.
Test if trainees know the commands. End with the “load” command.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Ending Questions (Theoretical)

Final Questions:
1. What’s one key rule you must always follow?
2. How should you treat other players?
3. What happens if you FRP?
Wrap up with these questions to reinforce rule knowledge.
###### Now we're done with this section, let’s go to the next section!

Results

Congratulations on completing the training!
- Pass: You are now cleared to join official patrols.
- Fail: Don’t worry, you can retake training after feedback.
Stand trainees in the result formation and deliver pass/fail messages.
